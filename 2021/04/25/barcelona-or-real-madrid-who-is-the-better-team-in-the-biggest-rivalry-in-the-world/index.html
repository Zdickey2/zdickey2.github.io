Barcelona or Real Madrid? Who is the better team in the biggest rivalry in the world?

2021-04-25

The world has many rivalries around the world, within countries, within leagues, even within divisions and cities. Everyone might not be into soccer but most people know of Barcelona and Real Madrid. Recently named the two highest valued teams in the world, Barcelona and Real Madrid have a rivalry that is unparalleled to any other rivalry in any other sport. In the last 10 years the rivalry has brewed with one of the greatest of all time Cristiano Ronaldo leaving Real Madrid. So I am going to look at which is the better team AND if Ronaldo leaving made an impact within this rivalry.

goals <- laliga %>%
  group_by(HomeTeam) %>% 
  summarise(
    Totalgoals = sum(FTHG), 
    OppGoals = sum(FTAG))
ggplot() + 
  geom_dumbbell(
    data=goals, 
    aes(y=reorder(HomeTeam, Totalgoals), x=Totalgoals, xend=OppGoals),
    size = 1,
    color = "grey",
    colour_x = "green",
    colour_xend = "red") + 
  theme_minimal()+
   labs(x="Total yards per game", y="Points per game", title="Barcelona overwhelming goal scoring", subtitle="Barcelona leads in the last 10 years in Goals scored and goals conceded..", caption="Source: LaLiga Santanger | By Zachariah Dickey") + 
  theme_minimal() +

    plot.title = element_text(size = 16, face = "bold"),
